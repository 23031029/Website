How to use our website

Step 1

Take our short 5-10 minutes assessment to get your own financial advisors

Step 2

Choose your own financial advisor among the ones given.

Step 3

After choosing a financial advisor, you can select the available date to have your first consultation for free

Step 4

If you're satisfied after the initial consultation, proceed to transfer funds to your financial advisor using our digital wallet.

Step 5

Book follow-up consultations by selecting "Booking," choosing a green date, and selecting a time slot and location.

Step 6

After about a month, review your digital wallet to see where your funds have been invested and your earnings for the month.
